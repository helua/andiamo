ANDIAMO is a directors duo consisting of Mat Erdmann and Michal Wieckowski based in WARSAW, Poland. We are juggling with the FORM, not shaping our works in any permanently. In love with creating RAW and GLARING worlds since always. Because being in BETWEEN doesn’t mean being NOWHERE, huh ?

We found JOY in mixing documentary and feature for such brands as: ADIDAS, SAMSUNG, PUMA, TYSKIE, BALLANTINE’S, WYBOROWA and VOGUE and such top polish artists as: PRO8L3M, BRODKA, PEZET, JJ ORLIŃSKI, KRÓL, SOKÓŁ and KAYAH.
